Chili

Ingredients: Ground beef/turkey. Lentils/beans (size~ kidney beans, chickpeas). Vegetables (carrots, celery, green beans; optional: corns, peas). Canned tomatoes. Chicken stock (with while wine). Chilli powder, Paprika, garlic powder, chilli flakes (if cayenne pepper: be EXTRA careful, a small amount can be super spicy). Optional: cumin, oregano, bay leaves, dried basil, salt & pepper.
Step 1: Brown the ground beef/turkey & stir-fry the veggies with a little bit of vegetable oil.
Step 2: In a pot, add beef/turkey & veggies & tomatoes & chicken stock. Bring to a boil.
Step 3: Reduce to simmer. Add seasonings accordingly, ~1-2 tbsp of each, except for cayenne pepper (AGAIN, be extra careful with cayenne pepper!) Simmer for at least 30 minutes

Carrot Cake

For 2 layers:
Wet ingredients: 3/4 cup brown sugar, 1/4 cup sugar, 3 eggs, 1/2 cup vegetable oil. (OPTIONAL: 1/2 cup crushed pineapple, or 1/2 cup apple sauce)
Dry ingredients: 2 cups cake flour, 2 tsp cinnamon, 1/4 tsp all-spice/5-spice, 1/2 tsp salt, 1 tsp baking powder, 1/2 tsp baking soda, 2 cups shredded carrots (~ 2 carrots), 1/2 cup crushed nut
Step 1: Food processor: shred 2 carrots, crush 1/2 cup nuts
Step 2: sift dry ingredients & mix (excluding carrots & nuts)
Step 3: hand mixer (at level 1): mix wet ingredients (slowly add vegetable oil last). slowly, multi-times add dry ingredients
Step 4: add carrots & nuts, mix
Step 5: 350F 25-30 min

Margherita Pizza

Ingredients:
DOUGH: slightly less than 1 cup all-purpose flour, half a packet of fleischmann pizza yeast, 1/3 cup of water (microwaved for 30s), 1 tbsp of oil. 1/2 tsp of sugar. 1/4 tsp of salt.
TOPPINGS: tomato paste/pizza sauce. fresh mozzarella cheese. fresh basil. cherry tomatoes (sliced into half).
Step 1: Make the dough: combine 2/3 cup of flour, yeast, sugar, salt in a bowl, water, and oil in a big bowl. Mix well (~1 min)
Step 2: Gradually add remaining flour to dough ball. Dough will be slightly sticky. Knead on floured surface (~4 min). Shape into 0.5 cm thick.
Step 3: preheat oven AND BAKING SHEET at 425F(220C). Add toppings. Bake for 12-15 minutes (closer to 12 because the dough is thin).

Butter Cookies

Ingredients (for ~12-15 cookies): 1/2 cup of unsalted butter. 1/4 cup of sugar. 1 egg yolk. 1 cup of all-purpose flour. Optional: matcha powder / 1 tsp of vanilla extract / diced dried cranberries.
Step 1: Melt butter over slightly boiling water. While mixing, add sugar, egg yolk, vanilla extract (if applicable). Sift flour and matcha powder (if applicable). Mix well.
Step 2 (if cylindrical shape): Form dough shape, wrap in plastic wrap. Chill in freezer for ~1 hour (flip halfway to freeze evenly). Unwrap & cut (with a sharp knife) into 1cm-thick cookie shape. (if piping tip) Add room temperature dough to piping bag, squeeze circular shape onto baking sheet.
Step 3: Bake on baking sheet at 350F for 12 minutes. Cool completely on rack.

Chicken Noodle Soup

Ingredients: chicken stock (original)，chicken, celery, carrots, frozen peas, pasta; oregano, garlic powder. Optional: rosemary, bay leaf
(optional step 0: brine chicken overnight)
Step 1: dice celery & carrots & chicken. cook veggies and chicken separately.
Step 2: Add veggies & chicken to chicken stock, bring to boil
Step 3: Add pasta & frozen peas, bring to boil again.
Step 3: Reduce to simmer, add seasonings & salt & pepper. Simmer until pasta is al dente.

Brownie

Ingredients (for 24 small pieces): 1/3 cup cocoa powder; 1/2 tbsp instant coffee; ~4 ounces (~100g) unsweetened/dark chopped chocolate; 1/2 cup + 2 tbsp (or 1/8 cup) boiling water; 4 tbsp (or 1/4 cup) unsalted butter; 1/2 cup vegetable oil; 2 eggs + 2 egg yolks; 1/2 tbsp vanilla extract; 1.5-2 cups sugar, 1 3/4 cups all purpose flour
Step 1: combine cocoa powder, instant coffee, 2 ounces chocolate, boiling water, mix; add butter, oil, eggs & egg yolks, vanilla extract, sugar, mix; add sugar, mix; add flour, mix; add 2 ounces chocolate, mix
Step 2: Pan with parchment paper, 350F 30-35min. Cool for 30 minutes before digging in - or not.

Foccacia

Ingredients: 2.5 cups bread flour; 1 tsp salt; 1.5 tsp active dry yeast, 1/2+1/3 cup lukewarm water, 1 tsp sugar, 2 tbsp olive oil
Step 1: In a large bowl, whisk flour + salt
Step 2: in a small bowl, mix yeast + sugar + 1/3 cup lukewarm water
Step 3: combine large & small bowls, add 1/2 cup lukewarm water + olive oil; mix into dough
Step 4: knead dough 5-10 min
Step 5: spread olive oil in a large bowl; rest dough in the bowl at a warm place for 1 hour
Step 6: take dough out of bowl, push air out, fold like 井, put back in bowl and rest for 30 min
Step 7: spread olive oil in baking sheet, stretch dough in baking sheet and rest for 30 min
Step 8: Topping: water-olive oil 1:1 mixture; rosemary; salt; halved cherry tomatoes (optional), other herbs (optional)
Step 9: 425F 25-30 min
Step 10: Last but not least: vinaigrette dressing

Hummus

Step 1: Cook 1/2 cup dry chickpeas
Step 2: In a blender: 1 cup cooked chickpeas, 1/2 cup sun dried tomatoes soaked in olive oil, 1/2 cup lemon squeeze, 3-5 cloves chopped garlic (or 2 tbsp garlic powder), 1 tbsp paprika

Granola

Ingredients: 2 tbsp coconut oil, 1/4 cup maple syrup/honey, oats, 1 tbsp tahini/peanut butter (for stickiness), vanilla extract (optional), cocoa powder (optional), matcha powder (optional)
Step 1: Combine & mix all ingredient in a saucepan over low heat
Step 2: Lay granola on baking sheet, 350F 25min

Banana Bread

Wet ingredients: 2 big ripe bananas, 2 eggs, 1 tsp vanilla extract, 1/4 cup melted butter
Dry ingredients: 2 cups whole wheat flour, 1/2 cup brown sugar, 2 tsp baking powder, 1 tsp baking soda, 1/2 tsp salt, (optional) chocolate chips, pecan, cinnamon
Step 1: Mix dry ingredients & wet ingredients (Save some sugar & melted butter for later) separately, then combine & mix
Step 2: Pour into greased loaf pan
Step 3: Sprinkle sugar & melted butter on top for a crispy top (optional)
Step 4: 350F 60min

Step 2: Combine wet ingredients
Step 3: Combine dry & wet

Baked Potato Wedges

Step 1: Cut potatoes into fries. Soak in water for 10min
Step 2: Dry with kitchen towel. Cover in olive oil, salt, pepper, etc.
Step 3: 425F 40-45 min. Turn over half way

Cornbread

Ingredients: 1 cup whole wheat flour, 1/2 cup cornmeal, 2 tbsp sugar, 1 tsp active dry yeast, 2 eggs/6 tbsp egg whites, 3/4 cup water, 1/4 cup oil, 1.5 cup defrosted corn
Step 1: Mix all ingredients together
Step 2: Rest 2 hr room temperature
Step 3: Mix to remove air
Step 4: Rest 30 min room temperature
Step 5: 350F 30min

Apple Crumble

For a loaf pan:
Bottom-layer ingredients: 2 apples, 1 tbsp lemon juice / squeeze half a lemon, 1 tbsp cinnamon, 1 tbsp sugar
Top-layer ingredients: 1/4 cup instant oats, 1/4 cup whole wheat flour, 2 tbsp butter (chopped into small cubes), 1 tsp cinnamon, 1/6 cup brown sugar, 1 tbsp chia seeds, 1/2 cup chopped nuts (TO TRY NEXT TIME)
Step 1: chop apples into small pieces, cook over medium heat with all bottom-layer ingredients until soft
Step 2: with hands, mix all top-layer ingredients
Step 3: 350F 25 min
